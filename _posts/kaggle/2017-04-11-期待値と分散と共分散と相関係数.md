# 期待値と分散と共分散と相関係数について

## 期待値

$$
E[X+Y] = E[X] + E[Y]
$$

$$
E[XY] = E[X] \cdot E[Y]
$$

## 分散

$$
V[X+Y] = V[X] + V[Y] + 2Cov[X,Y]
$$

$$
V[X-Y] = V[X] + V[Y] - 2Cov[X,Y]
$$

## 共分散

$$
Cov[X,Y] = E[(X-\mu_x)(Y-\mu_y)]
$$

$$
Cov[X,Y] = E[XY] - \mu_x \mu_y
$$

## 相関係数

$$
\rho = \frac{Cov[X,Y]}{\sqrt{V[X]V[Y]}}
$$
